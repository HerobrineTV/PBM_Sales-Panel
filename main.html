Tipps Pricing / Valuation

by bluethiccfish

1. Fame

Depending on your Fame you can set Prices.

0 to 100 Fame ~ 10 Robux

100 to 1K Fame ~ 25 Robux

10K to 50K Fame ~ 200 Robux

>50K needs to be determined by looking at more factors


2. Badges

A YouTuber, Verified, TikToker or Twitch Streamer badge tends to double or triple the value of cards, multiple badges is even better. developer or roblox mod badges are worth more than others.
